How might we improve the experience
of accessing information?

The context

Reports from news agencies over the last year underline the fact that young people are the most likely to be uninformed and dissatisfied with current media.

The reason lays on multiple factors: on one hand, traditional media, such as paper-based and newscast, are not able anymore to intercept the needs and expectations of younger; on the other hand, the web and digital media are perceived in relation to a general sense of distrust.

In particular, these latter are dominated by information pluralism, a concept according to which a vast aggregate of different information sources and points of view can lead citizens to come across manipulated, false or low-quality information.

The problem

Results from the research prove that young people perceive information as too biased due to opinionism, and they feel a sense of mistrust towards it due to the difficulty in recognizing how trustworthy a news really is.

As a consequence, getting a clear and true picture of what is going on becomes too demanding for people, especially if we consider the amount of time and cognitive effort this activity requires.

This frustration impacts people’s motivation and often turns in giving up.

Nevertheless, there is an internal inconsistency: young recognize that, after all, it is important to be aware of what is going on around them and in the rest of the world. Therefore, our assumption is that the right tools to get a satisfying experience in accessing news are missing.

The system functioning

The system combines the professionalism of a team of experts to certify the reliability of the sources, and the computational capabilities of artificial intelligence, which processes different articles from those same sources, and combines them into a single piece of content related to the same news.

To respond to the dynamic lifestyle of our target audience, this team designed an app as on-the-go touchpoint. In this way, our users - who moreover are digital natives - can access news at anytime and wherever they are.

Finally, we believe getting informed is a form of social commitment and can play an important role for users’ motivation. That’s why we decide to give users the possibility to relate their own informating activity with that of the other people.
