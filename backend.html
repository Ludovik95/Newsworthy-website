An information system that provides only one article for each relevant daily topic combining all reliable online sources

But technologically speaking: how it can really work?

NLP algorithms

Natural language processing (NLP) is a family of algorithm capable to process and analyze large amounts of natural language data.

In that project, that kind of algorithms has been exploited through two main tasks:

  • Natural language understanding (NLU): Finalized to the text analisys is able to convert input bunch of texts into spatial entities to be processed by the computer and to collect the semantic properties of each

  • Natural language generation (NLG): Convert information from computer databases or semantic intents into readable human language.

Newsworthy backend process

Articles scraping

Download all the articles of the day and their metadata (url, publishing date, authors, title, text) found in the URL of the newspaper webpage inserted as input.

Articles similarities

Firstly, understand the topic covered by each article from the sources; then group together the articles which talk about the same topic of the day.

Articles generation

For each group of articles compute an abstractive summarisation to generate a new article text, syntesis of all the relevant information, and generate for it a new title.
